Drought Risk Assessment Expert System

A Rule-Based Expert System for African Agricultural Communities

Project Purpose

This expert system is designed to classify drought levels and provide appropriate response recommendations for agricultural communities in Rwanda and across Africa. Using environmental, meteorological, and agricultural data, our system helps predict, identify, and manage drought situations before they become severe.

Drought has significant impacts on Rwanda's economy, food security, and livelihoods. With approximately 70% of Rwanda's population employed in agriculture, effective drought monitoring and response systems are crucial for protecting communities and ensuring food security.

African Context & Relevance

This system addresses critical needs in the African agricultural context:

  • Africa's vulnerability to climate change impacts, including increasing drought frequency
  • Rwanda's dependence on rain-fed agriculture
  • The need for localized drought assessments due to varied microclimates in Rwanda's hilly terrain
  • Limited access to meteorological expertise in rural communities
  • The importance of early warning systems to mitigate drought impacts

Key Stakeholders

  • Farmers (subsistence and commercial)
  • Local government authorities
  • Agricultural extension officers
  • Meteorological agencies
  • Disaster management organizations
  • NGOs involved in food security
  • Water resource management agencies
  • Rural communities

Expert System Approach

Our system uses rule-based reasoning with IF-THEN rules to:

  1. Collect and analyze key drought indicators (rainfall data, soil moisture, temperature trends)
  2. Classify drought severity levels
  3. Generate region-specific and context-appropriate recommendations
  4. Alert relevant stakeholders with actionable information

Example rules include:

  • IF rainfall_last_3_months < 50% of normal AND soil_moisture < 30% THEN drought_level = "Severe"
  • IF drought_level = "Severe" AND region_type = "Agricultural" THEN recommend = "Implement water rationing, provide emergency irrigation, distribute drought-resistant seeds"

Development Stage: Day 1
